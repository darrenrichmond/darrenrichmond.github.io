Welcome.

I moved to Denver a few years ago, and have fallen in love. The community I have met here is extremely supportive and provides constant encouragement to grow in new ways.

My main current project is Music Volunteers, a Code for Denver project that connects people sick or dying to volunteers who perform music. We are just getting started and I am volunteering as Product Manager. Aspects I am working on:

  • Fundamental assumptions and what would have to be true for them to be validated.
  • A questionairre to validate our assumptions with users and other stakeholders.
  • A minimum viable product to continue validating assumptions and test our hypotheses.
